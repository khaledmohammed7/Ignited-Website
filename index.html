About Root & Ripple

Pioneering the Future of Energy

Root & Ripple is an innovative energy solutions company headquartered at STEM Alexandria School. We are committed to revolutionizing renewable energy by integrating advanced technology into everyday household and commercial products. Our mission is to provide sustainable energy solutions that empower individuals and businesses to reduce electricity consumption, lower costs, and contribute to a greener planet.
We envision a future where energy efficiency is seamlessly integrated into daily life. By harnessing cutting-edge renewable energy sources, we aim to redefine how people interact with energy-efficient solutions in their homes, offices, and commercial spaces.
